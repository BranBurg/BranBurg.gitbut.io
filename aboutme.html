About me!

I want to share my story with you. I've had quite a journey in my career. My name is Brandon Burgener. I worked for a Sheriff’s Office as a Sergeant for several years, but I burned out of that line of work and wanted to find something different that I was passionate about. While searching, I got another offer to be a Program Manager for another agency to run their field training and academy program. After another year, I was ready to leave the state government and enter the corporate foray.

I joined Uber as a Senior Trust and Safety Investigator, and after some hard work, I was promoted to Global Talent Acquisition as a Recruiting Sourcer. I enjoyed my work and found professional happiness for the first time. I was able to look ahead and towards my future plans. In 2023, Uber announced layoffs and redundancies, and my entire team was affected. I took this opportunity to search again for what I wanted. I ended up where I should have always begun: information technology. I've always been interested in technology, from gaming to tinkering to enjoying the latest tech. I was always my family’s IT guy. After some deliberation, I figured that I should finally get paid for my services, and I am now pursuing my degree in Information Technology and Cyber Security.

This journey has taught me a lot, and I look forward to learning more. But there has always been something that holds me back—my fitness. Starting a fitness journey can be a daunting task, but it’s also an exciting one. It’s a chance to take control of my health and well-being and to make positive changes in my life. While pursuing my professional goal, I want to challenge myself and pursue my fitness goals. I believe that fitness is not just about looking good, but it’s also about feeling good. It’s about having the energy to do the things I love and to live life to the fullest. I'm excited to embark on this new journey and to see where it takes me. I know that it will not be easy, but I am ready to put in the work and make positive changes in my life. I’m excited to take control of my health and well-being and to inspire others to do the same. If you are ready to start your own fitness journey, I am here to help you every step of the way.
