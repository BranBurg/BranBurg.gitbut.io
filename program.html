Current Program

PPL (Push, Pull, Lift) Progam
Push Workout:
  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light aerobic activity
  • Barbell bench press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Incline dumbbell press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Seated military press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lateral raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Triceps pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Cable crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Pull Workout
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Seated cable rows: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Face pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
Legs Workout
  • Barbell squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Leg press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Romanian deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-12 repss
  • Leg curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Hanging leg raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Nutrition
  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and nuts
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with fruit
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with brown rice and vegetables
  • Snack: Apple with almond butter
  • Dinner: Salmon with sweet potato and asparagus
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple
Remember, this is just a sample workout and nutrition plan. It’s important to customize your plan based on your fitness goals, dietary needs, and personal preferences. If you’re new to working out, consider working with a personal trainer to help you create a customized plan that meets your specific needs.
